Code d'activité : VSIC 9311

Business Activity Code: VSIC 9311

VSIC 9311 — Hoạt động của các cơ sở thể thao (Exploitation d'installations sportives) — autorise 100% de capital étranger sans joint-venture obligatoire.

VSIC 9311 — Hoạt động của các cơ sở thể thao (Operation of sports facilities) — allows 100% foreign ownership with no mandatory joint venture.

  • Salles de sport et fitness Gyms and fitness centers
  • Courts de tennis, badminton, squash Tennis, badminton, squash courts
  • Infrastructures de sports de raquette (padel inclus) Racket sports facilities including padel
  • Complexes sportifs multi-activités Multi-activity sports facilities

Mise à jour VSIC prévue novembre 2025 (Decision 36/2025/QD-TTg). Le code 9311 est maintenu avec ajustements mineurs. À reconfirmer si ouverture après novembre 2025.

VSIC update planned November 2025 (Decision 36/2025/QD-TTg). Code 9311 maintained with minor adjustments. To confirm if opening after November 2025.

Qualification des coachs

Coach Requirements

La loi vietnamienne exige une qualification reconnue. Le padel n'a pas encore de cadre dédié au Vietnam — assimilé aux sports de raquette.

Vietnamese law requires recognized qualifications. Padel has no dedicated framework in Vietnam yet — treated as a racket sport.

Profile 1

Profil 2
Profile 2

Profil 3
Profile 3
Licence d'athlète avec classement reconnu Athlete license with recognized ranking

Solution pratique : un coach vietnamien certifié sport de raquette (volet légal) + un coach expat avec certification internationale (niveau technique).

Practical solution: one Vietnamese coach certified in racket sports (legal cover) + one expat coach with international certification (technical level).
